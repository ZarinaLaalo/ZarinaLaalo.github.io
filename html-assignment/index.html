About Me

Zarina is a 2nd child born in 1888 in the small town of Lollipop County. She was raised by a scurry of chipmunks until the age of 6, when she moved to Bean Town to pursue her passion of creating greeting cards. Several decades later, Zarina met her partner and they moved to Canada with nothing but a suitcase full of personalized pens.

"Everything is funny, if you can laugh at it." - Lewis Carroll

Zarina enjoys reading books outside in the rain, making ugly cakes, and sifting through fruits at the grocery store. After exploring a thrilling career in Exhibition Development for over a decade, Zarina now hopes to become the Queen of Web Design by mastering the art of HTML and CSS. This assignment is only the beginning.
