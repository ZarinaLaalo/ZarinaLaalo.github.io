ABOUT.

golden child creations. is the pseudonym for Ottawa based designer Zarina Laalo. The name is a play on her first name, which translates to "golden" while also describing her standards for excellence in design and production.

DESIGNER. CURATOR. STORYTELLER.

Zarina Laalo is a designer/curator with a bachelors in studio arts and a masters in art history. She has worked with museum and gallery exhibitions and public programming for over a decade. Zarina is currently a student in the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College.

Zarina's theory, knowledge, and experiences in arts and curatorial practices is integral to her creative mindset and design approach. She is studying to learn new technical skills to help create innovative digital solutions to the field of exhibition design, but is also interested in working with design agencies that take on creative, unconventional clients. She is particularly interested in projects that involve user engagement and crafting interactive digital experiences.
